Workflow

My sourcing process from idea to PO-ready

A transparent, repeatable process so you always know how I reach each sourcing recommendation.

1

Define buy box and criteria

We align on target ROI, net profit, price ranges, categories, marketplaces, and risk tolerance (competition, brand risk, seasonality).

2

Data-driven product research

I scan catalogs, storefronts, and supplier price lists using tools like Keepa and SellerAmp to shortlist SKUs with healthy sales rank and pricing history.

3

Supplier contact & quotes

I reach out to suppliers (or work from your existing list), request updated pricing, and compare quotes against our profitability requirements.

4

Profit & risk validation

For each candidate SKU, I calculate fees, landed cost, net profit, and ROI, while checking for competition levels, stock history, and potential brand issues.

5

Actionable sourcing report

You receive a clean spreadsheet or dashboard with buy/skip decisions, suggested order quantities, and notes for your final approval.
